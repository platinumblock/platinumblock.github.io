I Am A Designer.

Technology is the most important factor in our 21st century lives. I want to design a better world for all of us.
I am currently a 1st year student at the University of California, Berkeley, majoring in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science. Throughout my early years, I did a lot of programming - software engineering, website development, game development, and others. While the engineering medium changed, I always loved the design aspect.
I am obsessed with user experience. Every time I use an app or website that has a major flaw in design or usability, I am seriously annoyed. I'm motivated to create the smoothest, easiest, most effective user experience for technology possible.
A lot of people, in many different fields, say they want to make an impact through technology. However, the benefit of all technology comes from it being used effectively. I hope my work enables technology to be used for good, impacting the world.
I'm experienced in Figma and front-end development (HTML, CSS, JS, React). But I also have skills in user research, synthesis, ideation, and prototyping.
I am currently a member in two technology consulting clubs at UC Berkeley: UX at Berkeley, and Code For Good. I cherish the experiences and opportunities I've gotten from these groups.
